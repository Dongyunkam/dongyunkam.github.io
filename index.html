About

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Embedded Processor & Intelligent Computing (EPIC) LAB at EE, POSTECH. My research has focused on Energy-Efficient Digital System Design under the supervision of Prof. Youngjoo Lee. More specifically, my research interests include:

  • Hardware-software co-design and co-optimization
  • ASIC Implementation and Verification
  • Efficient Hardware architecture for DNN Inference
  • Low-bit quantizaiton and sparity-aware computing
  • Energy-Efficient AI deployment in edge devices
  • Next-generation wireless communication system
