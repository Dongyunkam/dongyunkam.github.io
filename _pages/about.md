I am a postdoctoral researcher at [POSTECH EPIC Lab](https://sites.google.com/view/epiclab/home).
As a hardware design engineer and researcher, I am interested in **energy-efficient hardware systems** for DNN inferences, wireless communications, and signal processing.
My research mainly focuses on **HW architectures, SW-HW co-optimizations, and ASIC/FPGA implementations** at the **digital circuit level**.

I received the B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees from POSTECH EE, Republic of Korea, in 2018, 2020, and 2024, respectively.
I was extremely fortunate to have been advised by [Prof. Youngjoo Lee](https://sites.google.com/view/epiclab/member/yjlee?authuser=0) while pursuing my PhD.

:computer: Research Interest
======
* Energy-efficient DNN inference accelerator
* LLM's efficient inference
* Error correction code (ECC) for mobile systems
* Wireless communication systems
* CPU/GPU/FPGA/ASIC implementation
